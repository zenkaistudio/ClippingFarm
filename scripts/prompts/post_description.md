# Social Media Post Description Formula

## The "Tonight/Runway" Viral Template

Observed pattern: this caption style consistently drives high engagement/virality on TikTok and Instagram. People copy it because it works algorithmically — multiple @mentions pull in fanbases, the editorial tone creates a pattern interrupt, and the aspirational close elevates the post.

### Formula
```
Tonight, [celebrity/subject] [did something notable] at [prestigious event or location/context].
[One sentence of additional context that elevates the subject or adds narrative depth].
[Close with an aspirational or culturally resonant reference — "runway", "history", "legend", etc.]
```

### Why it works
1. **"Tonight" opener** — signals live/breaking energy, creates immediacy
2. **Multiple @mentions of major accounts** — fires notifications into multiple large fanbases at once
3. **Prestige hashtag or location** — hooks the post into an already-trending topic pool
4. **Editorial/journalistic tone** — reads like a Vogue/entertainment-news article snippet, not a casual caption; pattern interrupt makes people slow down and actually read it
5. **Aspirational close** — ends on a culturally elevated word/phrase, leaves a positive emotional residue

### Example (real observed viral caption)
> "Tonight, thv took a turn in the audience as he watched @graciebrams and @dojacat perform at #VogueWorld: Hollywood. No stranger to a fashion show either, the @bts.bighitofficial star dressed in a look that could have easily appeared on the runway."

### Template variants by niche

**Streamer/gaming:**
> "Tonight, [streamer] broke the internet after [viral moment] during a live with [other streamer]. No stranger to [relevant context], the [platform] star delivered a moment nobody saw coming."

**Celebrity/entertainment:**
> "Tonight, [celebrity] [action] at [event]. No stranger to [field], the [descriptor] star [close with aspirational note]."

**AI/tech content (e.g. Higgsfield):**
> "Tonight, AI crossed a line nobody thought possible. No stranger to pushing limits, Higgsfield's latest generation left the internet speechless — straight off the runway."

## Usage in pipeline
When generating post descriptions alongside each final clip, use this formula with Claude, passing:
- `hook_title` (already generated by stage_detect_moments.py)
- `category` (emotional_peak, punchline, controversial_take, etc.)
- Streamer/creator name and any co-present names in the moment
- Platform context (TikTok vs IG Reels vs YT Shorts)

Output: a `suggested_caption.txt` file alongside each `final.mp4` in `output/`.
